CAUSES & SOLUTIONS
WHAT CAUSES AIR POLLUTION
1. Household and Farming Chemicals
2. Deforestation
3. Smoking
4. Industrial Emission
5. Open Burning of Garbage Waste
SOLUTIONS FOR AIR POLLUTION
1. Walk, bike, carpool, or take public transit.
2. Say no to wood fire burning.
3. Reduce, reuse, and recycle.
4. Turning off lights and appliances when they are not in use.
5. Buying fewer things that are manufactured using fossil fuels.
